About me

My passion is developing open-source software for understanding the spread and control of disease. I am one of the lead developers of HPVsim and Covasim, as well as of the Optima suite of models for HIV, TB, nutrition, and malaria. I hold a PhD in Mathematics from UNSW Sydney, and spent 7 years working within the University of Copenhagen's Data Science Laboratory. I am now an independent contractor based in Sydney, Australia.
